About Martta

As you can see, Martta is a cat. She has orange eyes and a grey fur. She looks an awful lot like a cat breed called british short hair, but she is not. She has a colorful personality and she is a very good and loyal furry friend.

The story of Martta

  • Martta was born in Turkey approximately in 2017
  • Mixed breed
  • Rescue cat, used to live on the streets
  • Martta was rescued from the streets in the summer of 2018

Martta today

  • Martta flew to Finland in February 2019
  • At first Martta was very small and skinny
  • After she was taken care of she started to gain some weight and strenght
  • Martta has now lived in Finland three years and she is happy and healthy!
  • Martta has also made new friends in Finland :)
